Citizen FAQ

Where should I start reading?

Start with Starfall Echoes, the first book in the main trilogy. It establishes the foundations of the Aethelgard System.

When is the next book release?

Solar Ascendant is currently in pre-order and scheduled for interstellar transmission on summer solstice 2026.

Are there signed copies available?

Limited signed editions of The Cosmic Chronicles are released quarterly to members of the Citizenry Vault. Check your dashboard for the next drop date.

Is there a community for fans?

Yes, the Citizenry Vault is our official community hub where fans discuss theories, share art, and decrypt hidden lore together.

Are audiobooks planned?

Audio transmissions for the entire Cosmic Chronicles are currently in production and expected to be available by late 2026.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, our physical transmission pods reach most star systems globally. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by sector.

Can I suggest content for future books?

While the main storyline is fixed, we run monthly community polls in the Vault to decide on minor world-building details and lore expansions.
